# Power Audit Checklist

::: info
Quick take: Work down this list in order. The first real gap you hit is usually the best next move. It is built to run fast on a screenshot or a quick look at someone's city, so you can give a straight answer to "what should I upgrade next?"
:::

## How to use this

Go top to bottom. Each step is a quick check and the fix. Stop fixing at the first clear gap — that is normally the highest-value action — but note everything you find so you can hand back a short ordered list. The order matches the [Power Priority](/strategy/power-priority) page: cheap permanent power first, gated and spend-heavy systems last.

## The audit

### 1. Research queue

- **Check:** Is something researching right now?
- **Fix:** If the queue is idle, that is the first thing to fix — it is free power going to waste. Queue the next Battle-tab node for their main troop type. Make sure Tool Enhancement and Tooling Up are maxed for their level first.

### 2. Building parity

- **Check:** Are any core buildings more than a level or two behind the Furnace?
- **Fix:** Bring the laggards up to Furnace level. It is usually free power, and often a requirement for the next Furnace step anyway.

### 3. Troop tiers

- **Check:** What is their top troop tier, and are lower tiers still sitting in the camps?
- **Fix:** Promote everything to the top available tier, then train into any unused capacity.

### 4. Chief Gear (Furnace 22+)

- **Check:** Are all six pieces crafted? What tier, and is it even across pieces?
- **Fix:** Craft any missing piece first to unlock the set bonuses, then enhance Infantry, then Marksman, then Lancer — kept close together.

### 5. Chief Charms (Furnace 25+)

- **Check:** Are any charms above level 1? Are they spread evenly, or spiked on a few?
- **Fix:** Spread evenly. Aim everything at level 11 to unlock the material exchange before pushing higher.

### 6. Heroes

- **Check:** Are their top combat heroes near max star for their generation, or are shards spread thin across the whole roster?
- **Fix:** Concentrate shards on the main trio, favoring attack and lethality heroes. Push the exclusive weapon on the carry.

### 7. Hero gear

- **Check:** Is the top heroes' gear keeping pace with their star level?
- **Fix:** Upgrade gear on the main heroes only.

### 8. Pets

- **Check:** Are the combat pets they actually field leveled?
- **Fix:** Level the fielded pets; skip the rest.

### 9. Experts and VIP

- **Check:** Are Dawn Academy experts progressing? Is VIP climbing?
- **Fix:** Steady progress only. These are the lowest priority on the list.

## Reading a screenshot fast

If someone sends a power breakdown or a city screenshot, three things tell you the most in five seconds:

1. **Furnace level versus the rest of the city** — parity gaps are instant free power.
2. **Top troop tier versus their Furnace** — are they promoted up to what they should already have?
3. **Chief Gear tier, and whether all six pieces exist** — the most common mid-game miss.

Everything else is refinement on top of those three.

## Before you tell them to spend

Two checks, so the advice holds up:

- **Permanent first.** Point them at research, buildings, and troop tier before any gated or paid system. It is cheaper, and it never gets lost.
- **Time it to SvS.** If the gap is a gated system — charms, gear enhancement, Fire Crystal upgrades, hero shards — and State of Power prep is close, the best advice is often "do it, but during prep." Same power, plus the points your state needs. See [Power Priority](/strategy/power-priority#bank-it-for-svs) and the [SvS Prep Checklist](/events/svs/prep-checklist).
