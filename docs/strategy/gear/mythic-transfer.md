# Mythic Transfer

::: info Quick take
Gear transfers between heroes of the same troop type are free. Infantry → Infantry, Lancer → Lancer, Marksman → Marksman. This is why investing in Molly's gear is also investing in Mia's gear — and why you should never hesitate to gear a transitional hero.
:::

## The Rule

Same-type gear transfers cost nothing. The gear moves immediately with all enhancement levels, Master Forgery levels, and stats intact. Cross-type transfers cost resources and should be avoided.

| Transfer Type | Cost |
|---|---|
| Infantry → Infantry | Free |
| Lancer → Lancer | Free |
| Marksman → Marksman | Free |
| Any cross-type (e.g., Infantry → Lancer) | Costs resources — avoid |

## Why This Matters

This mechanic makes every planned hero transition safe for your gear investment:

**Molly → Mia (Lancer):** Molly's Mythic Goggles at +45 MF2 transfer to Mia at +45 MF2. Zero cost. Every gem you spent on Molly's gear is now Mia's gear.

**Bahiti → Alonzo (Marksman):** Bahiti's Mythic Goggles at +52 MF3 transfer to Alonzo at +52 MF3. Zero cost.

**Sergey → Flint (Infantry):** Already executed on Beav's account. Sergey's Mythic Gloves and Belt transferred to Flint immediately when Flint arrived.

## When to Transfer

**Immediately when the replacement hero is roster-ready.** There's no reason to delay — the new hero benefits more from Mythic gear at any enhancement level than the old hero keeping it.

"Roster-ready" for a replacement hero means:
- 3★ minimum (for Mythic heroes)
- At least Lv3 Expedition skills
- Assigned as your primary for that troop type

## When NOT to Transfer

- **The replacement hero isn't ready.** A 1★ Alonzo with Lv1 skills is worse than a 4★ Bahiti even with Mythic gear. Keep gear on the active hero until the replacement crosses the readiness threshold.
- **You're not sure about the replacement.** If a new hero might not actually replace the current one, wait until you're committed.

## Common Mistakes

1. **Waiting too long to transfer.** If Flint is your primary Infantry and Sergey is benched, Flint should have the gear today — not next week.

2. **Investing in cross-type gear hoping to transfer.** An Infantry piece on a Marksman hero can't transfer for free. Don't build cross-type gear expecting a cheap move later.

3. **Not planning for transfers when choosing enhancement targets.** Molly's Goggles at +45 MF2 is a perfect stopping point because those exact stats transfer to Mia. Over-investing past +45 wastes resources on diminishing returns that Mia inherits anyway.
