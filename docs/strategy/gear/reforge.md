# Reforge Strategy

::: info Quick take
Reforge REPLACED pieces to fund enhancement on NEW Mythic pieces. Never reforge gear you're still using. Never reforge gear that could transfer to another hero for free.
:::

## What Reforging Does

Breaking down a gear piece returns a portion of its enhancement materials (Ore, Essence Stones) that you can use to enhance other pieces. The refund is partial — you don't get everything back — but it's the primary way to fund new Mythic piece enhancement without starting from zero.

## The Golden Rule

**Reforge replaced pieces → fund new Mythic pieces.**

When you replace an Epic +45 Gloves with Mythic Gloves, the old Epic is now useless on that hero. Reforge it. The recovered Ore and Stones go directly into enhancing the new Mythic piece.

## When to Reforge

- **You just upgraded rarity.** Old Epic replaced by new Mythic → reforge the Epic.
- **Spare gear in inventory.** Event rewards, excess drops → reforge for materials.
- **Hero permanently benched.** Their gear will never be used again → reforge. But check transfer eligibility first.

## When NOT to Reforge

- **Gear you're still using** — even temporarily. Don't strip your active hero to fund a future one.
- **Gear that could transfer to another same-type hero.** Lancer gear on Molly → transfers free to Mia. Marksman gear on Bahiti → transfers free to Alonzo. Don't reforge what you can transfer.
- **Mythic gear** — unless you're absolutely certain the hero is permanently benched AND the gear can't transfer. Mythic gear is too expensive to rebuild.
- **Gear on a hero you might reactivate.** Meta shifts happen. If there's any chance you'll need that hero again, keep their gear.

## Worked Example

Flint arrives from Lucky Wheel. Sergey (Epic Infantry) has Epic +46 Gloves and +45 Belt.

1. Transfer Sergey's Epic Gloves and Belt to Flint (free — same troop type)
2. Acquire Mythic Gloves for Flint via Arena tokens
3. The transferred Epic Gloves are now replaced by Mythic Gloves
4. Reforge the old Epic +46 Gloves → recover Ore
5. Use recovered Ore to enhance Flint's new Mythic Gloves toward +30

The old Epic funded the new Mythic's enhancement. Sergey keeps his secondary gear (Goggles, Boots) and gets demoted to bench or joiner role.
