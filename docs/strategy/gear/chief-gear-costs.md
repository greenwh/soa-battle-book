# Chief Gear Upgrade Costs

::: info Quick take
Chief Gear unlocks at Furnace 22 — a six-piece set that buffs all your troops regardless of which heroes you send. Upgrades cost **Hardened Alloy** and **Polishing Solution** early, add **Design Plans** from the Rare tier on, and add **Lunar Amber** at the top tiers. The tables below show the cost of each upgrade step, per piece and for a full six-piece set.
:::

## How Chief Gear Upgrading Works

Chief Gear is six pieces, paired by troop type — each pair buffs one troop type's Attack and Defense across every march you send, no matter which heroes lead. Keeping all six pieces at matching tiers unlocks set bonuses, so even progression beats rushing one piece far ahead.

Each piece climbs through tiers (Uncommon → Rare → Epic → Mythic), with star levels and T-sub-tiers inside the higher tiers. Different upgrade steps consume different materials, which is why some rows below show only one material.

Most accounts upgrade **Infantry pieces first** (they take the most damage), then Marksman, then Lancer. Once any single piece reaches **Mythic T2 with 3 stars**, you unlock the **Enhancement Material Exchange**, which lets you trade surplus materials for ones you need.

## Material Sources

- **Hardened Alloy** — Crazy Joe, Frostfire Mine, Level 22+ beasts and Polar Terror rallies, and the Alliance Championship Shop (1:1 with Championship Badges).
- **Polishing Solution** — Crazy Joe and Frostfire Mine, and the Alliance Championship Shop (100:1 with Championship Badges).
- **Design Plans** — many events, plus the Alliance Championship, Foundry, Power Region, and Canyon shops.
- **Lunar Amber** — Material Exchange and packs; needed only at the top tiers.

## Common Mistakes

1. **Letting tiers fall out of sync.** Set bonuses need matching tiers. One piece far ahead of the others wastes the bonus.
2. **Spending Design Plans randomly.** They gate everything from Rare on. Spend them on your priority troop type's pieces first.
3. **Ignoring the Enhancement Material Exchange.** Once it unlocks (any piece at Mythic T2 3★), it fixes material imbalances. Use it instead of grinding the one material you're short on.
4. **Upgrading during downtime instead of events.** Many events award points for gear upgrades. Time big upgrades for SvS, KoI, or Hall of Chiefs to double-dip as event score.

## Quick Reference

- **Unlocks at Furnace 22.** Six pieces, paired by troop type. Match tiers for set bonuses.
- **Materials:** Hardened Alloy + Polishing Solution early, Design Plans from Rare on, Lunar Amber at the top.
- **Order:** Infantry → Marksman → Lancer.
- **A set costs 6× the per-piece amount.**
- **Mythic T2 3★ on any piece** unlocks the Enhancement Material Exchange.
- **Time upgrades for events** (SvS / KoI / Hall of Chiefs) to score points.
