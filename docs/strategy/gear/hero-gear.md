# Hero Gear

::: info Quick take
Focus on priority slots by troop type, push to Mythic as fast as possible, and concentrate Master Forgery on one piece at a time. Mythic at +30 beats Epic at +50.
:::

## Slot-to-Stat Mapping

Every gear piece has a specific stat profile. Understanding this determines which slots to enhance first.

| Slots | Hero Stats | Troop Stats |
|---|---|---|
| **Goggles + Boots** | Hero Attack | Troop **Lethality** (offensive) |
| **Gloves + Belt** | Hero Health | Troop **Health** (defensive) |

## Priority Slots by Troop Type

Each troop type has two priority slots based on their combat role:

| Troop Type | Priority Slots | Reasoning |
|---|---|---|
| **Infantry** (Flint, Sergey) | Gloves + Belt | Tank role — Health keeps them alive longer |
| **Lancer** (Molly, Mia) | Goggles + Boots | Balanced but lean offensive — Lethality first |
| **Marksman** (Bahiti, Alonzo) | Goggles + Boots | Pure damage role — Lethality is everything |

Always enhance priority slots before secondary slots. A fully enhanced set of priority pieces with bare secondary pieces outperforms an evenly enhanced set.

## Rarity Tiers

Empty → Common (Gray) → Rare (Blue) → Epic (Purple) → Mythic (Orange)

Each rarity jump is massive — larger than many enhancement levels combined. **A Mythic piece at +30 dramatically outperforms an Epic piece at +50.** This means:

- Always upgrade rarity before pushing enhancement on a lower-rarity piece
- When you acquire a Mythic piece, replace your Epic immediately — even at +0 Mythic
- Reforge the old Epic to fund the new Mythic's enhancement (see [Reforge Strategy](/strategy/gear/reforge))

## Enhancement Strategy

Enhancement levels (+1 through +60+) add stats incrementally. The cost per level increases, and returns diminish at higher levels.

**Enhancement targets by priority:**

| Range | Value | Recommendation |
|---|---|---|
| +0 to +30 | Excellent | Push here immediately on any Mythic piece |
| +30 to +45 | Strong | Priority pieces should reach this ASAP |
| +45 to +55 | Good | Solid F2P target for primary heroes |
| +55 to +60 | Diminishing | Only for your #1 hero's priority pieces |
| +60+ | Low ROI | Whale territory — F2P should stop at +55 |

**Keep both priority pieces roughly even.** No reason to max one while the other lags — the stat gains are symmetric.

## Master Forgery (MF)

Master Forgery is an additional enhancement tier available only on Mythic gear. Levels 1–5, each providing significant stat bumps that compound on top of base enhancement.

**The golden rule:** Focus MF on one piece at a time. MF3 on one piece beats MF1 on three pieces.

| MF Level | Investment | F2P Recommendation |
|---|---|---|
| MF1 | Moderate | Get this on both priority pieces early |
| MF2 | Significant | Good F2P target for primary heroes |
| MF3 | Heavy | Practical F2P ceiling — strong returns |
| MF4–5 | Extreme | Whale territory — skip unless heavy spender |

MF uses Essence Stones, which come from Master Forgery events, KoI rewards, and limited sources. Don't waste them on secondary pieces or non-primary heroes.

## Widgets / Exclusive Gear

Mythic heroes unlock a 5th gear slot (exclusive weapon) at 1★ Ascension. Widgets provide Hero Attack/Defense/Health AND Troop Health/Lethality as **Special Bonuses** — meaning they multiply on top of base combat stats, not add to them.

| Widget Level | Troop Buff |
|---|---|
| 2 | 5% |
| 4 | 7.5% |
| 6 | 10% |
| 8 | 12.5% |
| 10 (Max) | 15% |

**Target: Level 6+ (10% threshold).** Widget upgrades require 275 total widgets per hero. Sources: Enigma events, Mystery Shop, packs.

For rallies, only the rally leader's widget applies. Joiners' widgets don't contribute.

## Common Mistakes

1. **Enhancing Epic gear past +45.** If you're approaching Mythic acquisition, stop enhancing Epic and save resources for the Mythic piece.

2. **Spreading MF across multiple pieces.** MF3 on Flint's Gloves alone is better than MF1 on Gloves, Belt, and Goggles.

3. **Enhancing secondary slots before priority slots are at target.** Your Boots and Goggles (for Infantry) can stay at Epic while your Gloves and Belt push to Mythic +55.

4. **Gearing joiner heroes.** If a hero's only role is rally joining, their gear does nothing. Save enhancement for heroes who lead rallies or fight in Arena.
