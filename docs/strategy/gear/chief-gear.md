# Chief Gear

::: info Quick take
Chief Gear is a separate equipment system for your Chief (not your heroes). Six pieces provide troop-type-specific Attack and Defense buffs, with set bonuses that reward uniform quality. Unlocks at Furnace 22. The set bonus caps at your lowest-quality piece — raise the floor, don't sprint single pieces.
:::

## Unlocks at Furnace 22

Chief Gear becomes available when you reach Furnace 22. If you're below F22, focus on [Furnace progression](/strategy/furnace) — Chief Gear is one of the major reasons to push for F22.

## Gear Pieces by Troop Type

| Troop Type | Pieces | Buff |
|---|---|---|
| **Infantry** | Coat + Pants | Infantry Attack & Defense |
| **Marksman** | Ring + Weapon (Cane) | Marksman Attack & Defense |
| **Lancer** | Cap (Helmet) + Watch | Lancer Attack & Defense |

Each piece buffs a specific troop type's Attack and Defense stats. The buffs apply to all your marches, not just a specific hero.

## Set Bonuses

This is the critical mechanic that most players miss:

| Requirement | Bonus |
|---|---|
| **3 pieces of same quality** | All Troop Defense bonus |
| **6 pieces of same quality** | All Troop Attack bonus |

The set bonus checks your **lowest-quality piece**. If five pieces are Epic quality but one is Rare, your set bonus is Rare-tier across the board.

**Consequence:** Craft all 6 pieces before enhancing any single one. Keep all pieces within 1–2 quality levels. A single laggard piece caps your entire set bonus.

## Quality Tiers

Uncommon → Rare → Epic → Legendary/Mythic

Higher quality requires rarer crafting materials and more time, but the stat jumps are substantial — especially the set bonus improvements.

## Upgrade Order

1. **Infantry (Coat + Pants)** — frontline survivability, benefits your primary tank
2. **Marksman (Ring + Cane)** — damage output, benefits your DPS hero
3. **Lancer (Helmet + Watch)** — balanced buffs, benefits your Lancer
4. **Raise all 6 to uniform quality** — set bonus is more valuable than any single piece

## Enhancement Materials

Chief Gear enhancement uses different materials than hero gear:

| Material | Sources |
|---|---|
| **Hardened Alloy** | Polar Terror rallies (Lv3+), Beast Hunting (Lv22+), Crazy Joe, Foundry Shop, AC Shop |
| **Polishing Solution** | Same sources as Hardened Alloy |

These materials are slow to accumulate. Don't spread them across multiple quality tiers — raise the floor uniformly.

## Chief Charms (Furnace 25)

At Furnace 25 you unlock Chief Charms — three slots on each gear piece that add **Lethality and Health** to the matching troop type. They are a major power layer in their own right, and one of the highest-scoring SvS prep tasks. See the full **[Chief Charms](/strategy/gear/chief-charms)** guide for the slot map, the Level 11 material-exchange breakpoint, Jewel Secret gates, materials, and SvS timing.

## Common Mistakes

1. **Enhancing one piece to max before crafting all six.** The set bonus at 6 uniform pieces is worth more than a single maxed piece. Craft all 6 first.

2. **Ignoring the quality floor.** One Rare piece among five Epics means your set bonus is Rare. That single weak piece is your highest priority upgrade.

3. **Forgetting to equip Chief Gear.** It sounds obvious, but new players sometimes craft gear and forget to equip it. Check your Chief equipment screen after crafting.

4. **Spending Charm Designs unevenly.** Uniformity bonus means balanced investment wins.
