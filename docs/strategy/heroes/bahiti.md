# Bahiti

::: info Quick take
A-tier for an Epic hero. Bahiti is the strongest Epic Marksman available and will serve your roster well until Alonzo (Mythic) is developed enough to replace her. The Epic ceiling is real — she can't compete with fully developed Mythics long-term — but she punches above her weight for the investment required.
:::

## At a Glance

- **Class:** Marksman (Epic)
- **Generation:** Gen 1
- **Acquisition:** Story progression, shard shop, events
- **Tier:** A-tier (for an Epic) · Placeholder until Alonzo

## Skills

### Key Skills

**Precise Shot** (Exploration) — Primary Arena damage. Push to Lv4 first.

**Quick Shot** (Exploration) — Secondary Arena damage. Push to Lv4 second.

**Pathfinder Vision** (Exploration) — Utility. Lv4 when manuals allow.

### Skill Priority

Bahiti's Exploration skills drive her Arena value. Push all three to Lv4 — this is one of the highest-impact Skill Manual investments available on a Gen 2 roster for Arena performance.

Expedition skills are lower priority since Bahiti isn't an ideal rally leader or joiner. Level them to Lv3+ for Labyrinth and general Expedition content.

## Gear & Enhancement Priority

**Priority slots:** Goggles + Boots (Marksman = pure damage = Lethality priority)

- Goggles + Boots → Hero Attack + Troop Lethality
- Gloves + Belt → secondary, Epic is fine

**Enhancement targets:**
- Priority pieces: Mythic, +52 MF2–3
- These pieces transfer to Alonzo (free Marksman-to-Marksman) when he's ready

## F2P Stopping Points

| Target | Priority | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| 4★ | High | Achievable through normal gameplay — Epic shards are plentiful |
| All Exploration skills at Lv4 | High | Single biggest Arena improvement |
| Goggles/Boots at +52 MF3 | High | Strong return, transfers to Alonzo |
| Beyond +52 MF3 | Low | Diminishing returns — save for Alonzo transition |

## Best Used For

- **Arena** — strong Exploration skills make her a competitive Arena pick
- **General Expedition** — reliable Marksman damage
- **Placeholder role** — keeps the Marksman slot warm until Alonzo is roster-ready

## The Epic Ceiling

Bahiti is Epic-rarity, which means she hits a hard ceiling that Mythic heroes don't face. Her base stats, skill caps, and gear options are all lower than a fully developed Mythic. She's excellent value for the investment — but she's not a forever hero.

Plan for Alonzo to replace her. When Alonzo hits 3★ with Expedition skills at Lv3+, he's ready to take over. Bahiti's Mythic Marksman gear transfers to him at zero cost.
