# Alonzo

::: info Quick take
S-tier when fully developed. Alonzo is the endgame Marksman for Gen 2 rosters — Captain Ahab's 15% rally lethality makes him an elite rally leader. But "fully developed" takes months of passive shard accumulation. Don't rush him; don't force gem spending on his shards until Mia's Lucky Wheel is secured.
:::

## At a Glance

- **Class:** Marksman (Mythic)
- **Generation:** Gen 2
- **Acquisition:** Hall of Heroes (primary), event shards
- **Weapon:** Captain Ahab — 15% rally lethality at max (excellent for rally leading)
- **Tier:** S-tier when developed · Not roster-ready until 3★

## Why He's Worth the Wait

Captain Ahab's weapon buff (+15% rally lethality at max) makes Alonzo one of the strongest rally leaders in the game for Marksman marches. Combined with strong Expedition skills (Onslaught for primary troop buff, Poison Harpoon for debuff), a fully developed Alonzo is a significant upgrade over any Epic Marksman.

## Development Strategy

**The key word is passive.** Alonzo's shard accumulation should not compete with other gem priorities.

**Current state (both accounts):** 0–1★, 1/6 slivers. Hall of Heroes purchases at minimal pace.

**Gem priority order:**
1. Mia Lucky Wheel fund (~175,500 gems) — **must be secured first**
2. VIP maintenance (10k gems/month)
3. Alonzo HoH purchases — only with leftover gems after 1 and 2

::: warning Beav account discipline
On Beav (strict F2P), HoH gem purchases for Alonzo are paused entirely until Mia's Lucky Wheel fund is complete. Alonzo development resumes after Mia is secured.
:::

## Skills

**Trapnet** (Exploration) — Signature AoE damage + immobilize. Strong Arena skill.

**Onslaught** (Expedition) — Primary troop buff. The skill that makes him a strong rally leader.

**Iron Strength** / **Poison Harpoon** (Expedition) — Support skills. Level after Onslaught.

### Minimum Before Activation

Alonzo is NOT roster-ready until:
- 3★ minimum (gates Lv4 skills and meaningful stat thresholds)
- Expedition skills at Lv3+
- Mythic gear transferred from Bahiti

Don't activate him prematurely. A 1–2★ Alonzo is weaker than a 4★ Bahiti with Mythic gear.

## Gear Plan

Alonzo inherits Bahiti's Mythic Marksman gear via free same-type transfer. No need to build a separate gear set.

**Priority slots:** Goggles + Boots (Marksman = Lethality priority)

The transition happens when Alonzo reaches 3★ and has enough skill levels to outperform Bahiti.

## F2P Stopping Points

| Target | Priority | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| 3★ | Gate | Minimum before activating — don't use him before this |
| 4★ | High | Full Lv5 skill access, strong stat threshold |
| Onslaught → Lv4 | High | Core rally leader skill |
| Captain Ahab → Lv6+ | Moderate | 10% rally lethality threshold |
| 5★ | Skip | Same rule as all Mythics — poor ROI at F2P spending |
