# Hero Roster

::: info Quick take
This is the index of every hero: which generation and class they belong to, and how you get them. For *who to invest in*, see the [Development Roadmap](/strategy/heroes/development-roadmap) and the individual hero pages. For *when* each generation unlocks on your server, see the [Server Timeline](/reference/server-timeline).

The one acquisition fact that drives planning: each generation has a single **Lucky Wheel hero**, and that hero is your gem-saving target for the generation.
:::

## How heroes are acquired

Heroes come in four rarities. Mythic (SSR) heroes are the generational power heroes; Epic and Rare heroes fill support, gathering, and rally-joining roles.

**Mythic (SSR) — three channels per generation.** From Gen 2 onward, each generation releases three Mythic heroes, one tied to each of these sources:

1. **Lucky Wheel** — one hero per generation. This is the gem-efficient target. Spin to 120 per event; stop accumulating around 4 stars unless you spend. See the Lucky Wheel principle on the [Hero Hub](/strategy/heroes/).
2. **Hall of Heroes** — one hero per generation can *only* be unlocked here, using Marks of Valor (bought with gems or from packs).
3. **Hall of Chiefs** — one hero per generation. These heroes also tend to surface in other places over time (Hero Rally, King of Icefield and SvS shops, daily deals), so they are the least "gated" of the three.

Older-generation heroes stay obtainable after their generation passes — through the VIP shop, Hall of Heroes back-catalog widget chests, and assorted event shops — just at a higher relative cost than the current generation.

**Epic heroes** come from the hero recruitment pools (Advanced Recruitment) and a few specific events (for example, Gina from Gina's Revenge / Hero's Mission). Most are gathering or support heroes; a few (Bahiti, Jessie, Sergey) are valuable combat or rally-joiner pieces well past the early game.

**Rare heroes** come from Basic Recruitment. They are gathering heroes — useful for resource runs, not for combat.

## How to use this page

1. **Find your generation** on the [Server Timeline](/reference/server-timeline), then read across this roster to see the three heroes you can chase.
2. **Identify the Lucky Wheel hero** for that generation — that is your primary gem-saving target.
3. **Decide on the Hall of Heroes hero** — it is gated, so if you want it, budget Marks of Valor early in the generation window.
4. **Check the [Development Roadmap](/strategy/heroes/development-roadmap)** before committing, so you build the heroes that match the events you actually contest.
