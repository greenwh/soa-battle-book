# Flint

::: info Quick take
S-tier overall. The strongest rally leader available on Gen 1/2 servers. His January 2026 Immolation rework gave him two elite Expedition skills, making him the centerpiece of any competitive roster. Poor rally joiner — excellent at everything else.
:::

## At a Glance

- **Class:** Infantry (Mythic)
- **Generation:** Gen 1
- **Acquisition:** Lucky Wheel (primary), event shards
- **Weapon:** Dragonbane (Legendary)
- **Tier:** S-tier rally leader · A-tier Arena · B-tier rally joiner

## Skills

### Exploration (Arena / Hero Combat)

**Fires of Vengeance** — Primary Arena damage skill. Scales attack% and damage-taken debuff. Push to Lv5 after reaching 4★.

**Incinerator** — AoE damage. Good Arena skill, Lv4 is sufficient.

**Heat Diffusion** — Utility/support skill. Keep at Lv3–4. Low priority for Skill Manuals.

### Expedition (World Map / Rally / Troop Combat)

**Pyromaniac** (1st Expedition skill) — Gives troops a 20% chance to set enemies on fire with DoT damage. Chance-based, not a flat buff. This is why Flint is a poor rally joiner — this skill wastes a joiner buff slot.

**Burning Resolve** (2nd Expedition skill) — +25% all-troop Attack. S-tier rally leader skill. The #1 Lv5 target on any Gen 2 roster (requires 4★ gate).

**Immolation** (3rd Expedition skill) — Post-January 2026 rework: flat +25% Lethality for all troops. Previously RNG fire DoT — now a guaranteed flat buff. This rework made Flint the undisputed best rally leader on Gen 1/2.

::: warning Ignore older guides
Any guide published before January 2026 that calls Immolation unreliable or chance-based is outdated. The rework changed it from RNG to a flat +25% Lethality buff. Flint now has two S-tier Expedition skills (Burning Resolve + Immolation) — no other Gen 1/2 hero matches this.
:::

## Gear & Enhancement Priority

**Priority slots:** Gloves + Belt (Infantry = tanking role = Health priority)

- Gloves + Belt → Hero Health + Troop Health
- Goggles + Boots → Hero Attack + Troop Lethality (secondary priority)

**Enhancement targets:**
- Priority pieces (Gloves/Belt): Mythic, +55 minimum, MF3+
- Secondary pieces (Goggles/Boots): Mythic when available (Arena token grind), +40
- Widget (Dragonbane): Level 6+ (10% troop buff threshold)

**Gear transfer:** When Flint arrives, transfer Sergey's Infantry gear immediately — same-type transfer is free.

## Best Used For

- **Rally leading** — all modes. His Expedition skill suite (Burning Resolve + Immolation) makes him the #1 rally leader on Gen 1/2 servers.
- **Arena** — strong Exploration skills. Push Fires of Vengeance and Incinerator to Lv4+.
- **Expedition / Labyrinth** — full Expedition skill suite applies, dominant performance.
- **Garrison leading** — excellent tank with Infantry stat profile.

## Avoid For

- **Rally joining** — Pyromaniac (first Expedition skill) is chance-based. Wastes a joiner buff slot. Send Jessie or Jasser instead.
