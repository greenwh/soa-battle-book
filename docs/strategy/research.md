# Research (Technology)

::: info
Quick take: Research is the best power in the game — permanent, never lost in battle, and paid mostly in time rather than money. The only real limit is hours per day, so the single biggest mistake is letting the queue sit idle. Keep your research-speed and construction-speed nodes maxed, then pour the Battle tab into the troops you actually field. Hold research speedups and Fire-Crystal-for-research for SvS prep.
:::

## Why research is the top power lever

Every research node adds power that can never be taken from you, and the Battle tree converts straight into combat stats — troop attack, defense, health, and lethality. Unlike troops, research survives every war. Unlike gear and heroes, it is almost entirely time-gated rather than spend-gated, which makes it the highest-value place to put effort on a free or low-spending account.

That is why a strong research base often separates two accounts at the same Furnace level. The number on the city is similar; the one with deeper tech wins the fight.

## The one rule: never let the queue sit idle

The Research Center runs **one project at a time.** Your throughput is capped at the hours in a day plus whatever speedups you add. An empty queue overnight is research you can never get back — roughly a third of a day's progress gone if you sleep eight hours on an idle queue.

Two habits follow from the single queue:

- **Queue something before every logout.** Even a long tech you will not finish soon is better than nothing running.
- **Sequence beats speed.** Because you can only run one thing, *what* you research first matters more than how fast you clear it. Pick the node that helps most now, not the cheapest one.

## The three trees

| Tree | What it covers | When it leads |
| ---- | -------------- | ------------- |
| **Growth** | Research speed, construction speed, training batch size and speed, march count, healing | Early, and never fully stops |
| **Economy** | Resource production and gathering speed | Early game, then maintenance |
| **Battle** | Troop attack, defense, health, lethality by type; march and rally capacity | Mid-game forward |

## Priority for a mid-game account

By mid-game your economy is mostly built, so the order shifts toward combat — but two Growth nodes stay maxed forever because they speed up everything else.

1. **Keep Tool Enhancement maxed** (research speed). This is the most important node in the game. Every level makes every future research faster, so it pays for itself many times over. Max it for your level before anything else.
2. **Keep Tooling Up maxed** (construction speed). The same logic for buildings — a permanent discount on every upgrade you will ever do.
3. **Push the Battle tab for the troops you field.** Front-load infantry defense and health (your line has to hold), then your damage type's attack and lethality. If you run a marksman line, that is marksman attack and lethality.
4. **Regimental Expansion** (Battle tab) raises your march and rally troop capacity — more troops per fight means more damage or defense, so it is high value once your stats are solid.
5. **Capacity and support nodes as needed** — Camp Expansion (bigger training batches), Trainer Tools (faster training), Command Tactics (more simultaneous marches). These keep your army and economy flowing without being raw power.

A note on lethality: most of your account naturally fills up with Attack and Defense, while Lethality stays scarce. Where the Battle tree offers lethality, it is worth more than the same percentage of attack. (See [Chief Charms](/strategy/gear/chief-charms) for the other half of that story.)

## Fire Crystal research (War Academy, Furnace 30)

When your Furnace and Research Center both reach 30, you unlock the **War Academy** and a new research tier paid in **Fire Crystals.** This is the largest block of combat-power research in the game, and it is where most of your troop strength comes from in the late game. It tracks the same Fire Crystal tiers as your buildings, so plan it alongside your Furnace FC path — see the [Fire Crystal guide](/strategy/fire-crystal) for the cost shape.

## Keep the Research Center near your Furnace

The Research Center gates Furnace progression — several Furnace levels require a matching Research Center level. Keep the two within a level of each other so a lagging Research Center never blocks your next Furnace upgrade.

## Common mistakes

- **Idle queue.** The single biggest waste in the game. Always have something running.
- **Burning long speedups on short tech.** Don't spend a stack of speedups to finish a fifteen-minute research — wait for alliance helps on the short ones and save speedups for the long, high-value nodes (and for SvS).
- **Going wide instead of deep.** Spreading research thin across every node leaves you mediocre everywhere. Finish the lines that matter for your troops.
- **Neglecting Tool Enhancement.** Skipping the research-speed node to rush a combat tech slows down every future research. Max it first.
- **Letting the Research Center fall behind the Furnace** and discovering it blocks your next upgrade.

## Bank it for SvS

Research is a points goldmine during State of Power prep, and most of it you would be doing anyway:

- **Research speedups** score on the construction/research prep days.
- **Fire Crystals spent on War Academy research** score heavily during prep.

So hold your research speedups and your Fire-Crystal research pushes for the prep window when you can. The one thing you should **not** hold is the queue itself — research time is free and not points-gated, so keep something running between events at all times. Bank the speedups, never the queue. See the [SvS Prep Checklist](/events/svs/prep-checklist) for which day each one scores.

## Quick reference

- Never let the queue sit idle — throughput is one project at a time.
- Max Tool Enhancement (research speed) and Tooling Up (construction speed) for your level, always.
- Mid-game: Battle tab for your fielded troops — infantry survivability first, then your damage type's attack and lethality.
- Regimental Expansion for bigger marches and rallies.
- At Furnace 30, War Academy Fire Crystal research is the biggest combat-power tier.
- Keep the Research Center within a level of the Furnace.
- Bank research speedups and FC-for-research for SvS prep; never bank the queue.
