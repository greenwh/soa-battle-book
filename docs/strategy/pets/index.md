# Pet Strategy

::: info Quick take
Pets provide two buff layers: passive stats from leveling and refinement (always active), and active skills you manually trigger before events (2-hour duration). The combat pets are a major competitive advantage — don't neglect them.
:::

## How Pets Work

Pets unlock at Furnace 18 (Beast Cage building) and become available based on server age and generation progression. Each pet provides:

1. **Passive Stats** — from leveling and refinement, always active. Pet Leveling → Troop Attack and Defense bonuses. Pet Refinement → Troop Lethality and Health bonuses.

2. **Active Skills** — manually activated, 2-hour duration, approximately 20-hour cooldown. These are the combat buffs you pop before events.

## Utility Pets (Quick Reference)

Not every pet is about combat. These utility pets provide quality-of-life benefits:

| Pet | Skill | Value |
|---|---|---|
| **Cave Hyena** | Construction speed buff | Activate before every big upgrade — saves hours |
| **Musk Ox** | Resource gathering buff | Gates Giant Tapir (Gen 2 pet chain) — level to 15 first |
| **Arctic Wolf** | Stamina recovery buff | Nice to have, lower priority than combat pets |

**Musk Ox is the gate pet.** Reaching Musk Ox Lv15 unlocks Giant Tapir (Gen 2), which provides a passive pet food engine. This makes Musk Ox the highest utility-pet priority.

## Key Principles

**Refinement uses Wild Marks — don't waste the good ones.** Common Wild Marks go on Gen 1 utility pets. **Save ALL Advanced Wild Marks for Cave Lion, Saber-Tooth Tiger, and Frost Gorilla** (Gen 3–4 combat pets). Refinement is RNG-based and mats are NOT recoverable.

**Pet skills auto-activate in Labyrinth.** In Cave of Monsters and Gaia Heart zones, your combat pet skills trigger automatically. One less thing to remember.

**Active skills last 2 hours.** Time activation for the event window — don't pop them casually. See [Pet Buffs](/combat/pet-buffs) for where they work and the AC registration trap.

## Where To Start

**Just unlocked pets?** Level Musk Ox to 15 to unlock the Gen 2 pet chain. Then start leveling Cave Lion.

**Preparing for a competitive event?** Read [Combat Pets](/strategy/pets/combat-pets) for which pets to activate and in what order.
