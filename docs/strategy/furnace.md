# Furnace 20→30 Guide

::: info Quick take
Furnace 20 to 30 costs over 2.3 billion Wood, 2.3 billion Meat, 467M Coal, and 118M Iron across all required upgrades — plus 313 days of base construction time. Plan ahead, never let your queue sit idle, and prioritize prerequisite buildings so you're never blocked.
:::

## Why This Range Matters

Furnace 20–30 is where the game shifts from "build what you can" to "plan or stall." Each level gates major system unlocks:

| Furnace Level | Unlock |
|---------------|--------|
| 20 | Mastery Forging (Essence Stones for gear enhancement) |
| 22 | Chief Gear (6-piece combat gear set for your chief) |
| 25 | Chief Gear Charms (additional stat layer) |
| 30 | Fire Crystal upgrades (prestige levels, T5 troops) |

Missing any of these unlocks puts you behind players at the same power level who planned their Furnace progression. Chief Gear at F22 is especially important — it's a major combat stat jump that you want as early as possible.

## Prerequisite Pattern

The prerequisites follow a predictable rotation:

- **Embassy:** Required at every Furnace level. Keep it matched to your current Furnace level at all times. This is the single most important supporting building.
- **Troop Camps:** Infantry, Marksman, and Lancer camps rotate. Each appears as a prerequisite roughly every 3 Furnace levels. Upgrade all three in rotation — neglecting one creates a surprise block.
- **Research Center:** Appears at F20 and F28. Less frequent but still important to keep within range.

**Planning tip:** Before starting a Furnace upgrade, check what prerequisite building is needed for the *next* Furnace level too. If you have a second construction queue, you can upgrade the next prerequisite while the Furnace is building.

## Pacing Strategy

### For F2P / Minimal Spend Players

The biggest bottleneck isn't resources — it's time. At F26+, individual upgrades exceed 20 days of base time. Strategy:

- Never let your construction queue sit idle. An empty queue is unrecoverable lost time.
- Request Alliance Help every single day — it's free time reduction.
- Stockpile speedups from Bear Trap, daily missions, and events. Use them on Furnace upgrades and direct prerequisites only — these are your highest-value targets.
- Resource gathering heroes (Jessie, Natalia, and others) should have gathering skills prioritized during this phase. The ROI is direct.
- Upgrade resource production buildings (Sawmill, Coal Mine, Hunter's Hut, Iron Mine) to at least Lv20 for steady income.

### For Light Spenders

The second construction queue is the single most impactful purchase for Furnace progression. Two simultaneous queues compress months of sequential building into a much shorter window. If you're going to spend on anything during this phase, the permanent second queue is it.

Beyond that, the same F2P principles apply — speedups on Furnace, prerequisites ahead of time, alliance help daily.

### The F25–F28 Wall

Most players stall between F25 and F28. The reason is usually not resources but an unmet prerequisite they didn't anticipate. Embassy 25→26 alone takes 6 days base time, and you need it done *before* you can start Furnace 26→27.

Avoid this by always looking two Furnace levels ahead and pre-building the prerequisites.

## Resource Planning

The resource costs roughly double every 2–3 Furnace levels. Some benchmarks:

| Furnace Level | Wood + Meat (combined, M) | Perspective |
|---------------|--------------------------|-------------|
| 20→21 | 42 | One good gathering day |
| 24→25 | 120 | A few days of focused gathering |
| 27→28 | 280 | A week+ of gathering |
| 29→30 | 480 | Multiple weeks of stockpiling |
| F30 (final) | 600 | Plan for this early |

Don't farm level-by-level. Farm for the next milestone unlock (F22 for Chief Gear, F25 for Charms, F30 for Fire Crystal). Having a target keeps the grind motivated.

## After F30: Fire Crystal

Reaching Furnace 30 unlocks Fire Crystal upgrades (FC1–FC5 initially, FC6–FC10 in older states). These require Fire Crystals instead of standard resources. Fire Crystals come from Intel Missions, Arena shops, and events like Castle Battle.

Fire Crystal levels provide massive stat boosts and new building skins. They're the true endgame progression track.
