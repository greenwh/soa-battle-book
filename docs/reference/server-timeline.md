# Server Timeline

::: info Quick take
Every server starts at **Day 0** and unlocks content on a fixed schedule tied to **server age** (days since the server opened). New hero generations arrive roughly every **80 days**. Pets, Fire Crystal levels, and major events have their own milestones. Knowing your server age lets you save resources before a new generation drops instead of spending into the old one.

Timing varies by server. Treat every day number here as **approximate (±7 to 21 days)** — your server unlocks faster or slower based on how quickly it clears the required missions.
:::

## How to read this page

Server age is not shown directly in the game, so you estimate it. Once you know it, this timeline tells you what is unlocked, what is next, and roughly how long until it arrives.

The single biggest use: **stop spending hero or Fire Crystal resources right before a generation or FC milestone.** A new generation usually outclasses the one before it, so resources poured into the older generation lose value fast.

## How to find your server age

Three methods, from most to least reliable:

1. **VIP login days.** If you joined the server soon after it opened and have logged in every day to claim VIP points, your VIP login day count is a close estimate of server age.
2. **Monument "Beast Hunt" task date.** Open your Monument near the Arena and Fishing Hut. Find the date shown on the Beast Hunt task. Subtract about **6 days** to estimate the server's creation date, then count days to today.
3. **First President date (Sunfire method).** If your server has held at least one Sunfire Castle battle, go to Battle History → Hall of Fame and find the date of the first President. Add about **54 days** to get your current server age. (Sunfire Castle itself opens around Day 53–54, so the first President is elected close to that point.)

All three are rough. Keep your own notes when new content lands — that gives you the most accurate reference for predicting the next unlock.

## Fire Crystal milestones

The Fire Crystal Age opens at Day 60 (you must be at Furnace 30 to use it). Levels then unlock in steps:

| Day | Fire Crystal milestone |
| --- | --- |
| 60 | Fire Crystal Age unlocks (Furnace 30 required) |
| 150 | Fire Crystal 5 + Crystal Laboratory |
| 220 | War Academy + Fire Crystal Lab 5 (6 refines/day) |
| 315 | Fire Crystal 8 + Fire Crystal Lab 6 (7 refines/day) |
| 500 | Fire Crystal 10 |

FC6 and FC7 fall between FC5 and FC8; the per-level upgrade costs and prerequisites are on the [Fire Crystal guide](/strategy/fire-crystal).

::: warning Emerging content
The classic timeline tops out at **Fire Crystal 10 (Day 500)**. The oldest servers have begun receiving **Fire Crystal 11–12 and T12 troops** (T12 also arrives with Gen 10 / Flame Tech around Day 680). Server-age timing for FC11–12 is newer and less settled than the milestones above — verify against your own server before planning around it.
:::

::: tip Shard rotation
From Day 195, several reward sources (Intel Missions, Hero Hall keys, Hero's Mission, Fortress chests) shift to award shards for heroes **two generations behind** the current one. As your server ages, the "free" hero you can build toward keeps moving forward with it.
:::

## Common mistakes

1. **Spending into an outgoing generation.** Finishing a hero days before its replacement arrives wastes shards and gear materials. Check this timeline first.
2. **Assuming exact dates.** Servers drift ±7 to 21 days. Use the day numbers as a planning window, not a countdown.
3. **Rushing Fire Crystal before Furnace 30.** The Fire Crystal Age needs Furnace 30. See [Furnace 20→30](/strategy/furnace).
4. **Ignoring the pet snapshot for events.** Pet buffs lock in at registration for some events — unlock and activate the pet before you register, not after.
