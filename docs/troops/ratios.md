# Troop Ratios Reference

<Callout type="info">

**Quick take:** Recommended Infantry/Lancer/Marksman splits for every major in-game activity. Ratios are role-specific (Rally Leader vs. Joiner vs. Garrison vs. Solo). Some are generation-sensitive — flagged where applicable.

</Callout>

## How to read this

Three numbers per row: **Infantry / Lancer / Marksman**. They always sum to 100%.

Roles abbreviated:
- **LEAD** — rally leader (the player who starts the rally)
- **JOIN** — rally joiner (players who reinforce the rally)
- **GARR** — garrison (defensive deployment to a building)
- **SOLO** — solo march (single-player attack with no rally)

## Bear Trap (PvE)

| Role | Inf<br>Lan<br>Mar | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| Lead | **0<br>10<br>90** | Pure PvE. Bear has fixed HP and no counter triangle, so maximize lethality. Joiners bring the infantry meat shield. |
| Join | **20<br>20<br>60** | Pre-Gen 8 balance point: some infantry to absorb hits and stabilize lancer/marksman skill triggers. |

<Callout type="tip" title="Generation-sensitive">

**Pre-Gen 8:** Keep at least 5,000 infantry in the lead march as a safety floor. The 0% lancer can stretch to 15–20% pre-Gen 8 if your lancer lethality is close to marksman. Joiners commonly run 25–30% lancer to stably proc lancer hero buffs.

**Gen 8+:** Push to 0/15/85 or pure 0/10/90 DPS for lead. Joiners shift to 10/15/75 as marksman lethality pulls ahead.

</Callout>

## Notes

- These ratios assume your **hero composition** matches your troop type — i.e. Infantry-led marches with Infantry primary heroes, etc. If your heroes are mismatched, the ratio adjustments are minor and the right answer is to fix the heroes, not the troops.
- **Joiners** in any rally should slot the rally leader's preferred composition. Some alliances enforce join compositions; most just ask for "balanced."
- **3-hero march loadouts** (lead + 2 secondary) follow the same ratio logic — the lead's troop type determines the dominant ratio.
