# Rally Joining

::: info Quick take
Send the hero with the best first Expedition skill for the rally type. If you don't have the right hero, send troops with no hero — an empty slot is better than a wrong buff.
:::

## The Only Thing That Matters

When you join a rally, only **one thing** counts: your first hero's **first Expedition skill** (top-right skill in the Expedition skill tree). That's it. Not your hero's level. Not their gear. Not their other skills. Just the first Expedition skill.

The rally selects the top 4 highest-level first Expedition skills from all joiners. Your job is to make sure yours is one of them — or to stay out of the way.

## Best Joiners by Rally Type

### Offensive Rallies

Bear Trap, attacking rallies, Castle/Fortress attack, SvS battle phase.

| Hero | First Expedition Skill | Effect | Tier |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Jessie** | Stand at Arms | +25% all damage dealt | S |
| **Jasser** | (equivalent) | +25% all damage dealt | S |
| **Jeronimo** | (equivalent) | +25% all damage dealt | S |
| **Seo-Yoon** | (equivalent) | +25% troop Attack | A |

Jessie, Jasser, and Jeronimo all provide the same buff type: +25% to all damage dealt. This is mechanically stronger than a flat Attack buff because it multiplies the final damage output including other buffs.

Seo-Yoon provides +25% troop Attack, which is a stat buff rather than a damage multiplier. Still useful but mathematically weaker than the damage-dealt buff.

### Defensive Rallies

Garrison defense, reinforcing allies, Castle/Fortress defense.

| Hero | First Expedition Skill | Effect | Tier |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Patrick** | (defensive) | +25% troop Health | S |
| **Sergey** | (defensive) | +20% damage reduction | A |

Patrick's Health buff increases your garrison's total HP pool. Sergey's damage reduction reduces incoming damage. Both are strong defensive contributions.

## Heroes to AVOID as Joiners

These heroes are excellent rally **leaders** but waste a joiner buff slot:

**Flint** — His first Expedition skill (Pyromaniac) gives troops a 20% chance to set enemies on fire, dealing damage over time. That's a chance-based effect, not a guaranteed buff. His best skill, Burning Resolve (+25% all-troop Attack), is his **second** Expedition skill — it only counts when he's the rally leader.

::: tip Flint rework note (January 2026)
Immolation was reworked to grant flat +25% Lethality (no longer RNG). This makes Flint an elite rally leader with two S-tier Expedition skills (Burning Resolve + Immolation). But Immolation is his **third** Expedition skill, so the rework benefits him as a leader, not a joiner. Ignore any guide older than January 2026 that calls Immolation unreliable.
:::

**Natalia** — Her first Expedition skill is a chance-based stun. Stuns can help in PvP but aren't reliable enough to justify using a buff slot over a guaranteed +25% damage buff from Jessie or Jasser.

## The "Send Troops Only" Rule

If you don't have Jessie, Jasser, Jeronimo, Seo-Yoon, Patrick, or Sergey at a competitive skill level:

**Send your march with no hero at all.**

Your troops still fill rally capacity. More troops = more HP = more damage absorption = longer rally lifespan for everyone. That's a real contribution.

A blank hero slot is neutral. A wrong hero with a weak or chance-based first Expedition skill actively wastes one of the rally's 4 buff slots.

## Skill Leveling Priority for Joiners

Since only the first Expedition skill matters for joining, focus your Skill Manuals on that one skill:

- Jessie: Stand at Arms → Level 4 minimum, Level 5 if possible
- Patrick: equivalent defensive skill → Level 4+
- Sergey: equivalent defensive skill → Level 4+

Don't spread Skill Manuals across a joiner hero's other Expedition skills — those only matter if the hero leads.

## Common Mistakes

1. **Sending Flint as a joiner.** This is the #1 mistake in most alliances. Flint is your best leader but a terrible joiner.

2. **Sending your highest-power hero instead of your best joiner.** Power level doesn't determine joiner value. A Level 59 Jessie with Stand at Arms Lv4 is more valuable than a Level 69 Flint.

3. **Not checking hero slot order before joining.** The game sends your slot-1 hero by default. Make sure your joiner hero is in slot 1, not your gathering or Arena hero.

4. **Sending a wrong hero at Level 5.** A Level 5 first Expedition skill permanently locks into the rally's skill pool unless another Level 5 replaces it or the player is kicked. This is the most damaging mistake possible — it can't be undone and it wastes a buff slot for the entire rally.

## Quick Reference

| Situation | What to Send |
|---|---|
| Offensive rally, you have Jessie/Jasser/Jeronimo | Send them in slot 1 |
| Defensive rally, you have Patrick/Sergey | Send them in slot 1 |
| You have the right hero but low skill level | Send them — a low buff is better than no buff |
| You have no joiner hero | Send troops only, no hero |
| You're not sure | Send troops only — safer than guessing wrong |
