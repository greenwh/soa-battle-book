# Pet Buffs

::: info Quick take
Pet combat skills provide major stat boosts — but only in specific modes. The most critical thing to know: in Alliance Championship, buffs lock in at registration. Activate pets BEFORE you register your march.
:::

## Where Pet Buffs Work

| Mode | Pet Buffs? | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| Bear Trap / Bear Hunt | ✅ Yes | See joiner note below |
| Expedition (PvP marches) | ✅ Yes | Full combat buffs apply |
| Crazy Joe | ✅ Yes | |
| Foundry Battle | ✅ Yes | |
| Labyrinth | ✅ Yes | Auto-activate in Cave of Monsters and Gaia Heart zones |
| SvS Battle Phase | ✅ Yes | Time activation for Castle Battle window |
| Alliance Championship | ✅ Yes* | **MUST activate BEFORE registration** |
| Arena | ❌ No | No buffs of any kind work in Arena |

## The AC Registration Trap

::: warning This is the most common competitive mistake in the game
Alliance Championship snapshots your buffs at the moment you register your march. If you register without pet buffs active, you will NOT have them during the battle phase — even if you activate them afterward.
:::

**Correct AC workflow:**

1. Activate pet combat skills (Cave Lion, Saber-Tooth Tiger, etc.)
2. Activate gem buffs if using them
3. Set troop ratio to 50/20/30 (NOT the default 33/33/33)
4. **THEN** register your march
5. **Do NOT re-register** after buffs expire — re-registering captures your current (now buff-less) state and wipes the original snapshot

This applies to all timed buffs, not just pets. Gem buffs, alliance facility buffs — anything active at registration is captured. Anything that expires between registration and battle is lost if you re-register.

## Bear Trap Joiner Note

When joining a Bear Trap rally:
- Your pet combat buffs do **NOT** boost rally damage
- Only your first hero's first Expedition skill contributes to the rally's damage output
- Pet buffs help you carry more troops (deployment capacity via Snow Ape) but don't increase the rally's damage

The rally **leader** is where pet combat buffs pay off. If you're leading, activate everything. If you're joining, pets mainly let you bring more troops.

## Titan Roc Warning

Titan Roc's active skill (Armor Rift) reduces enemy HP by 5%. In most PvP this is useful. In Bear Trap, it can hurt you.

**If your alliance reliably kills the bear with time remaining:**
Do NOT use Armor Rift. It lowers the bear's maximum HP, which means your alliance deals less total damage, which means a lower damage score for everyone.

**If your alliance does NOT kill the bear within the time limit:**
Armor Rift is safe to use — reducing the bear's HP helps you get closer to a kill, and you weren't going to max the damage score anyway.

## Activation Timing

Pet combat skills last **2 hours** with approximately **20-hour cooldown**. Don't pop them casually — time activation for the event window.

For events with predictable timing (AC, Foundry Battle, Bear Trap), activate 5–10 minutes before the event starts so you have the full 2-hour duration.

For SvS Castle Battle, activate at the start of the battle window (typically 10:00 UTC Saturday). Use 12-hour gem buffs for SvS to cover the full window.

## Common Mistakes

1. **Registering AC march before activating pet buffs.** Buffs are snapshotted at registration. No buffs at registration = no buffs in battle.

2. **Re-registering AC march after buffs expire.** This overwrites your buff-captured registration with a buff-less one. Never re-register unless you've re-activated all buffs first.

3. **Using Titan Roc Armor Rift when alliance kills the bear easily.** Lowers bear HP = lower damage score = worse rewards for everyone.

4. **Assuming pet buffs work in Arena.** Nothing works in Arena — no pet buffs, no gem buffs, no alliance buffs. Only hero level, stars, Exploration skills, widgets, and hero gear affect Arena performance.

5. **Wasting the 2-hour duration.** Activating Cave Lion at 3 PM for a 6 PM event means it's expired before the fight starts. Time your activation.
