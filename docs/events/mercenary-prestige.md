# Mercenary Prestige

::: tip
**Quick take:** Monthly stamina-grind event. Scout Phaethon Mercenaries on the world map, defeat them solo or via rally, push through 50 waves of escalating difficulty. Includes alliance Captain bosses for shared rewards. Heavy stamina consumption (~1,110 stamina to clear all 50 waves), but failed solo attacks refund their stamina — making it a genuinely F2P-friendly progression event with great Hero Shard, Gem, and resource rewards.
:::

## At a Glance

- **Frequency:** Monthly
- **Duration:** Multi-day window (typically 4–5 days)
- **Eligibility:** All active players; no specific Furnace requirement
- **Activation:** Always-on during event window — start when ready
- **Why it matters:** Hero Shards (including Mythic), Gems, Speedups, Hero EXP, and resource bundles. Alliance Captain rewards for team participation. Difficulty unlocks across multiple events compound for better rewards over time.

## How It Works

The event has two sides — **Personal Challenges** (your 50-wave gauntlet) and **Alliance Challenges** (Phaethon Captain boss fights).

### Personal Challenges — The 50-Wave Gauntlet

When the event opens, you select **one difficulty tier and one difficulty level** for the whole event. The choice locks for that event — you can't change difficulty mid-event.

**Difficulty structure (4 tiers × 5 levels = 20 difficulty configurations):**

| Tier | Sub-levels |
|---|---|
| **Champions** | Easy → Normal → Hard → Nightmare → Insane |
| **Epic** | Easy → Normal → Hard → Nightmare → Insane |
| **Legends** | Easy → Normal → Hard → Nightmare → Insane |
| **Fearless** | Easy → Normal → Hard → Nightmare → Insane |

Each level above Easy must be **earned by completing the previous level in a prior event**. The minimum to unlock the highest difficulty is roughly **20 events** of consistent participation. This is a multi-year progression for late-game players.

::: warning Wave count varies by tier
The wave count depends on the difficulty tier you select. At **higher difficulty tiers, the personal ladder runs 25 mercenaries** rather than 50, escalating in difficulty with **no mini-bosses in the ladder** — the every-10 mini-bosses described below apply to the longer lower-tier ladders. Alliance Captain bosses are a separate track regardless of tier. Confirm your selected tier's structure in-game when the event opens.
:::

**The 50-wave loop:**

1. **Scout** a Phaethon Mercenary from the Event tab (costs **15 Stamina**)
2. The mercenary appears on your world map. You have **1 hour** to defeat it.
3. Choose one of three combat options:
   - **Attack solo** — additional 10 Stamina, fastest
   - **Rally** — additional 25 Stamina, slower but more powerful
   - **Request Help** — uses 1 Mercenary Contract (you start with 5)
4. Win → reward sent via in-game mail, next wave unlocked
5. Lose → solo Stamina refunded; rally Stamina is *not* refunded
6. Defeat all 50 waves to unlock the final reward and the next difficulty level

**Every 10th wave (10, 20, 30, 40, 50) is a mini-boss** with significantly higher power than surrounding waves. Plan ahead — these often require rallies even if you've been soloing.

::: tip Stamina refund on failed solo attacks
This is the most important F2P-friendly mechanic in the event. **If you solo attack and lose, your 10 Stamina is refunded** when your march returns. The 15 Stamina for scouting is *not* refunded. So you can experiment with hero/troop compositions cheaply on tough waves — try a few comps until something works, only paying the scout cost.

The same does **not** apply to rallies. If a rally fails, all participants lose their stamina.
:::

### Alliance Challenges — Phaethon Captains

As your alliance progresses through the event (enough members defeat enough high-level Phaethon Soldiers), R4 or R5 leaders unlock the ability to scout **Phaethon Captains** — alliance boss enemies with massive HP.

**How Captains work:**

- R4/R5 scouts a Captain → mail goes to all alliance members
- **30-minute timer** starts to defeat the Captain
- Any alliance member can send individual marches to chip away at HP
- Defeating the Captain rewards **all participants**
- Each Captain can only be defeated **once per event**
- If timer expires without defeat, the Captain can be re-scouted (one more attempt)

**The "1-troop test attack" trick:** When an alliance Captain is announced, the first wave of attackers should send only 1 troop (or a tiny test march). This ensures every alliance member gets a chance to land a hit (and thus qualify for rewards) before the Captain dies. After everyone has hit at least once, the strongest players can finish the kill.

## Stamina Management — The Core of the Event

The total stamina to clear all 50 waves of a difficulty is approximately **1,110 stamina** when going solo and winning every fight on the first try. In practice, expect 1,300–1,800 stamina across an event accounting for failed attempts and rallies.

**Where stamina comes from during the event:**

| Source | Stamina |
|---|---|
| Natural regeneration | 1 stamina per 5 minutes (288/day) |
| Stamina Cans | Variable — 50 to 200 per can typical |
| Daily missions and event rewards | Small allotments |
| Patrick's Stamina Cans (Expert: Agnes Optimization) | Up to 40 extra stamina twice daily |

**Multi-day events mean natural regeneration alone can fund most of the gauntlet** if you start early and play consistently. A 4-day event with full stamina cap usage gives roughly 1,150 stamina — barely enough for one full clear without Stamina Cans.

### Gina's Endurance Training — The Critical F2P Hero

**Gina (Epic Marksman) reduces Stamina cost on rallies and marches she's part of by up to 20%** at max skill level. For Mercenary Prestige, where you're spending 1,000+ stamina, that 20% reduction translates to ~200+ saved stamina per event.

**Use Gina in every rally and solo march where she's still strong enough to win.** Once enemies outscale her, swap to your main marksman lead — but transfer her hero gear to extend her viable range as long as possible.

::: warning Gina's stamina reduction only applies to marches she's in
The reduction is per-march, not global. If Gina is benched, you pay full stamina cost. The skill applies to **rallies and marches** Gina participates in, not to the **Scout** cost of 15 stamina (that always applies regardless of hero choice).
:::

## Rewards

Three reward streams overlap:

### Per-Wave Rewards (mailed after each defeat)

Each Phaethon Soldier defeated drops a reward by mail — the value scales with wave number and difficulty tier. Higher-tier difficulties give significantly more gems, hero shards, and speedups per wave.

### Stage Rewards (final clear bonus)

After the event ends, you receive a separate reward based on your highest wave cleared in the chosen difficulty. **Wave 50 clear** unlocks the top reward bracket. Rewards are **not cumulative** — you only get the reward for your highest wave milestone.

Example reward types (varies by tier):
- **Mythic Hero Shards**
- **Epic Hero Shards**
- **Gems** (large allotments at Wave 50)
- **Mythic Skill Manuals**
- **Speedups** (training, construction, research)
- **Hero EXP**
- Resource bundles

### Alliance Captain Rewards

Defeating a Phaethon Captain rewards every participating alliance member. Rewards include **alliance chests, additional Mercenary Contracts, gems, and shards**. Generally smaller per-Captain than your wave rewards, but stack across multiple Captains per event.

## Phase by Phase

### Pre-Event (1–2 days before)

**Save Stamina.** Stop using Stamina Cans on routine activities. Don't burn stamina hunting beasts or starting rallies the day before. A full stamina bar (1,000 cap typical) plus banked Stamina Cans is your starting position.

**Set up your roster.** Confirm Gina's Endurance Training is at max level (skill 5). Confirm your main marksman lead has competitive gear. If you've been planning to transfer hero gear from another marksman, do it before the event.

**Coordinate with alliance.** Mercenary Prestige needs alliance Mercenary Contract help-requesting. Confirm with R5/R4 the schedule for Captain attempts.

### Event Day 1 — Volume Day

**Lock in your difficulty tier and level.** Choose what you cleared last event, or one tier higher if you've been progressing. Don't pick higher than you can clear — failing the final tier means worse Stage Rewards.

**Start with Gina-led solo attacks.** First 10–15 waves are usually trivial. Burn through them with Gina to maximize her stamina-reduction benefit before she outscales.

**Don't rally easy waves.** Rallies cost 25 stamina vs. 10 for solo. Save rallies for waves you can't solo.

### Event Day 2-3 — The Mid-Tier

**Swap to your main marksman around wave 15–25** when Gina hits her power ceiling. Transfer her hero gear to your marksman if needed.

**Use Request Help on the every-10 mini-bosses.** You start with 5 Mercenary Contracts, plus +1 per assist you give to other alliance members. Don't waste Contracts on regular waves — save them for boss waves you can't solo.

**Help alliance members with their requests.** Each rally you join earns +1 Contract back. Cap is 7 bonus rewards per day from helping.

### Event Day 3-4 — The Final Push

**The 50-wave climb.** Final 10–15 waves typically require rallies. Coordinate with alliance for joiners. Pre-prep formations.

**Save Stamina for the final mini-bosses.** Wave 50 is the heaviest fight of the event. Don't burn yourself out at wave 47 and miss the final Stage Reward.

**Participate in every Phaethon Captain.** Captain HP is shared by all attackers. Send at least one test march per Captain to qualify for rewards. The "1-troop test attack" trick ensures everyone gets credit before stronger players finish.

### Post-Event

Stage Rewards mail within 24 hours. Difficulty unlocks for next event are automatic. Mercenary Contract assist bonuses are tallied per-day, not at end.

## Strategy by Player Type

### F2P / Minimal Spend

Mercenary Prestige is one of the most F2P-friendly events:

1. **Use Gina to her ceiling.** Her 20% stamina reduction is alliance-changing for this event. Skill up to Lv5 at minimum.
2. **Solo first, rally only when forced.** Solo costs 10 stamina, rally costs 25. The 15-stamina-saving math compounds across 50 waves.
3. **Use the failed-attack refund.** When the game says "outcome difficult to ascertain," try anyway. Failed solo attacks refund their stamina — only the 15 scout stamina is consumed. This lets you experiment with hero/troop comps cheaply.
4. **Don't push difficulty too hard.** Failing the Stage Reward at a higher difficulty means worse final reward than clearing a lower difficulty. **Clear what you can, advance one tier at a time across events.**
5. **Help with alliance requests.** Every assist gives +1 Contract back, plus stage rewards if your participation contributes to a Captain kill.

### Light Spender

Stamina Can packs — even 1–2 mid-event — extend your wave progression by 10–20 waves. The reward jump from clearing waves 40 → 50 is significant.

### Heavy Spender

Multiple Stamina Can packs and Frost Star packs enable pushing two tiers above your usual difficulty. Insane-tier rewards are noticeably better than Nightmare. The cost-to-reward ratio gets thin at the highest tiers — most spenders treat Mercenary Prestige as a moderate investment event.

## Common Mistakes

1. **Picking a difficulty too high.** Failing to reach Wave 50 means worse Stage Rewards than clearing Wave 50 at a lower difficulty. The "Stage Reward" is not cumulative — your highest reached wave in your chosen difficulty determines it.

2. **Ignoring Gina's Endurance Training.** Players with maxed Gina save ~200 stamina per event. That's 20+ extra wave attempts. F2P players who skip Gina are leaving real progress on the table.

3. **Rallying easy waves.** 25 stamina vs. 10. If your solo win rate is high, rally only when the game says you can't solo.

4. **Not using the failed-attack refund.** Many players see "outcome difficult to ascertain" and rally instead of trying. The solo attack refund means experimenting is nearly free — only the 15 scout stamina is consumed on a fail.

5. **Forgetting to help alliance Mercenary Contract requests.** Each assist returns +1 Contract to you. Help with up to 7 per day for the bonus reward chests too.

6. **Going offline during a 60-minute scout window without a plan.** If you scout, request help, and go offline — and no one rallies in 60 minutes — you've burned 15 stamina for nothing. Either confirm an alliance member is going to rally, or stay online to manage it.

7. **Rushing to alliance Captain without coordinating the 1-troop test.** When a Captain is scouted, the first attackers should send minimal forces. If a whale solo-kills a Captain in 30 seconds, alliance members who didn't get a hit miss the rewards.

8. **Using Stamina Cans before the event opens.** Save them. Mercenary Prestige consumes more stamina than any other regular event. Stamina Cans used on routine beast hunting are wasted optimization.

9. **Not transferring Gina's gear when she outscales.** When Gina's power ceiling is hit (usually around wave 20–25), transferring her hero gear to your main marksman extends her stamina-saving usefulness for several more waves. The temporary swap is worth the gear transfer cost.

10. **Forgetting the 30-minute Captain timer.** When R4/R5 scouts a Captain, you have 30 minutes. Players who see the mail an hour later have missed it. Set notifications, watch alliance chat actively during event days.
