# Bear Trap

::: tip
**Quick take:** Twice-weekly alliance PvE event. Rally against a Raging Bear that doesn't fight back — zero troop losses, pure reward event. Essence Stones, XP Components, and hero-gear materials drop based on damage dealt. Not skipping this is one of the highest-value habits in the game.
:::

## At a Glance

- **Frequency:** Activated by R5/R4 every ~47.5 hours; each player has a 2-natural-day personal cooldown
- **Duration:** 30-minute event window
- **Eligibility:** Any alliance member after the alliance builds the Hunting Trap (requires HQ, which requires 20+ alliance members and Lv.5 alliance)
- **Two traps available:** Since January 2025, alliances can build a second trap for different timezone coverage
- **Why it matters:** Essence Stones (hero gear upgrades), XP Components (gear enhancement), zero troop losses — the bear doesn't fight back

## How It Works

The R5 or R4 sets the trap (manually or by schedule). When the trap activates, a Raging Bear spawns near the alliance HQ and the 30-minute event window begins. Players rally against the bear; only **rally attacks** score real damage — solo marches are useless.

**The bear doesn't attack back.** This is the most important mechanic to internalize. There is no defensive requirement, no tanking, no hospital pressure. Every troop you send returns home alive. This is pure offensive scaling — your only job is to deal as much damage as possible in 30 minutes.

Rewards are tier-based on personal damage dealt. Higher damage = better chest tier = more Essence Stones, more XP Components, more hero-gear materials.

### The Rally Leader vs. Joiner Asymmetry

This is the rule everyone gets wrong, and it determines whether you score well:

**Rally Leaders contribute ALL THREE of their heroes' Expedition skills.** That's up to 9 skills if all three heroes are Mythic. Their Hero Gear, Chief Charm, Chief Gear, pet buffs, and minister buffs all apply.

**Rally Joiners contribute ONLY the first Expedition skill of the hero in their first slot (far left).** That's it. Hero Gear, Chief Charm, pet buffs, minister buffs — none of it matters for the rally damage. Just that one skill on that one hero.

The game then picks the **top 4 highest-skill-level joiner contributions** to apply to the rally (in addition to the rally leader's full kit). So in a 15-player rally, only 4 joiners' skills make it into the buff stack, plus the leader's 3 heroes — 7 skill contributions total decide the rally's damage output.

::: warning Joiner choice is the single most impactful decision
The hero you put in slot 1 as a joiner is the only thing that matters. If you put a defense-focused or HP-buff hero there at skill Lv5, you've locked a useless buff into the rally for everyone. Pick a +25% damage skill (Jessie, Jasser, Jeronimo) or don't slot a hero at all.
:::

### Trap Enhancement

The trap itself can be upgraded with Explosive Arrowheads (from Intel Missions and beast looting). Each level grants an alliance-wide damage boost against the bear. Maxing the trap to **Lv.5 gives +25% troop attack** against the bear for everyone.

The R5/R4 should announce trap times so members can donate arrowheads before activation. Trap enhancement resets after each event.

### The Cyrille Expert (August 2025+)

If you've unlocked Experts (Furnace 25+), Cyrille is the dedicated Bear Hunt Expert. Maxed she provides:

- **+30% Bear Hunt Damage Points** (self only)
- **+300,000 Bear Hunt Rally Capacity**
- **+30,000 Bear Hunt Troop Deployment Capacity**
- **+5 Essence Stones** per hunt
- **+5×100 XP Components** per hunt

For Bear Hunt-active players, Cyrille is one of the highest-priority Experts after Agnes.

## Scoring

Personal rewards scale with damage dealt. Each tier crossed unlocks better rewards. The actual damage thresholds vary by server age (newer servers have lower bars), but the principle is universal: **more damage = better chest**.

Primary rewards across all tiers:

| Reward | Use |
|---|---|
| **Essence Stones** | Hero gear Mastery Forging — primary reason to do Bear Trap |
| **XP Components (100/500/etc.)** | Hero gear enhancement (sacrifice into another piece for XP) |
| **Hero gear chests** | Random-tier hero gear |
| **Sergey shards** (newer servers) or **Lucky Hero Gear chests** (older servers) | Account growth |
| **Arrowheads** | Future trap enhancements |

Cyrille adds 5 Essence Stones and 500 XP per hunt as a flat bonus on top of damage-based rewards.

## Phase by Phase

### Before the Trap Activates

**R5/R4 schedules the trap.** Most alliances run on a fixed cadence — e.g. every other day at 14:00 UTC + 02:00 UTC for the second trap. Confirm your alliance's schedule.

**Donate arrowheads.** Anyone can contribute Explosive Arrowheads to enhance the trap. Maxing it to Lv.5 (+25% damage) before activation is the alliance's highest-leverage prep. Get into the habit of dumping arrowheads as you accumulate them.

**Save the right teams.** Set up at least 3 ready-made formations:
- **Formation 1 — Rally Leader:** Your best 3 Expedition heroes, all maxed skills if possible. Marksman lead with damage-stacking secondaries.
- **Formation 2 — Joiner with Jessie/Jasser/Jeronimo (or your alliance's standard joiner hero) in slot 1.** Other slots filled with anything to maximize troop count.
- **Formation 3 — Joiner with a low-skill hero in slot 1** (like a 1★ blue hero). For when you want to join a rally that already has good buffs locked in and don't want to overwrite someone's Lv5 Jessie. Anything with skill level ≤4 won't replace a Lv5 in the top-4 selection.

### During the 30-Minute Event

**Rally rotation is everything.** Top alliances run continuous rallies — when one finishes, another starts immediately. Joiners cycle between rallies as their troops return home.

**Rally Leaders go first.** Your strongest accounts should lead rallies. Their full 9-skill kit drives damage scaling. If your account is mid-tier and you're trying to lead, the alliance loses output — let the whales lead.

**Joiners fill rallies.** As soon as a rally appears, fill it. Empty joiner slots are wasted damage potential.

**Pet skill timing.** Pre-activate Snow Leopard's "Lightning Raid" (+30% march speed, ±5% troop lethality reduction on enemies) before joining a rally. You won't need it for survival — the bear doesn't fight back — but the march speed matters for cycling between rallies.

**Use Cyrille's skill.** If you have her, activate her Bear Hunt buff at the start of the event. Stays active for the duration.

### After the Event

Personal rewards distribute based on damage dealt. No claim button — they appear in your inbox automatically. Trap enhancement level resets to 0 for next time.

## Strategy by Player Type

### F2P / Minimal Spend

You can score well in Bear Trap without spending. Three priorities:

1. **Get a Lv5 first-skill hero for joining rallies.** Jessie (Epic Marksman) is the single most accessible — she comes from the standard hero pool and her +25% damage skill at Lv5 is alliance-changing. Bahiti, Jeronimo, Jasser, and Seo-yoon all also have +25% first-skill damage and are common F2P picks.
2. **Save Skill Manuals for first-skill heroes.** A Lv5 Jessie first skill is worth more in Bear Trap than two Lv3 skills on different heroes. Concentrate.
3. **Keep an arrowhead bank.** Donate to every trap. The Lv.5 trap enhancement (+25% damage alliance-wide) compounds across every rally for the rest of the alliance's existence.

You don't need top-tier heroes to lead rallies as F2P — let stronger accounts lead, and just make sure your **slot 1 joiner hero** is correct every single time.

### Light Spender

- Consider acquiring Cyrille as your second Expert (after Agnes)
- VIP shop-bought Skill Manuals on a +25% damage hero are high-value
- A Mythic Marksman lead-rally hero is the biggest single damage upgrade

### Heavy Spender

- Lead every rally you can
- Max Cyrille's skills first; she compounds with every Bear Trap forever
- Optimize your full Mythic Expedition skill kit on lead heroes — all 9 skills matter
- Marksman gear enhancement and Mastery Forging compound especially well here

## Common Mistakes

1. **Joining rallies with a defense-focused hero in slot 1.** Patrick, Sergey, Giny, and other defense/HP heroes have first skills that buff defense or health — useless against a non-attacking bear. Their Lv5 buff *replaces* a Lv5 damage buff in the top-4 selection, sabotaging the rally.

2. **Solo-attacking the bear.** Solo marches deal microscopic damage. Every troop deployed without joining a rally is wasted output.

3. **Skipping the alliance-wide trap enhancement.** Donating arrowheads is one of the highest-leverage actions in the game. The +25% Lv.5 enhancement applies to every rally, every player.

4. **Filling secondary hero slots with damage heroes when you're a joiner.** Slots 2 and 3 for joiners only contribute troop capacity. Put your highest-troop-count heroes there, not your best damage heroes.

5. **Putting a Lv4 +25% damage hero in slot 1 when others have Lv5.** A Lv4 +25% skill won't replace a Lv5 +25% in the top-4 selection — but it might replace a *different* Lv5 buff already there. Confirm your skill levels and coordinate with alliance.

6. **Lower-power players leading rallies.** It feels generous to "let everyone lead," but rally leader strength is the backbone of damage. Rally leadership should go to the strongest accounts.

7. **Sending troops with no hero at all because "you don't have a good one."** Sending no hero is worse than sending Jessie at Lv4, but better than sending Patrick at Lv5. Empty hero = empty buff slot = no negative impact. A wrong hero overwrites a good buff.

8. **Forgetting to enhance the trap on a tight schedule.** If the trap activates without enhancements, the alliance loses up to 25% damage that event.

## Quick Reference

| Action | Why |
|---|---|
| Donate Explosive Arrowheads to the trap before activation | Lv.5 = +25% alliance-wide damage |
| **Joiner slot 1: Jessie / Jasser / Jeronimo / Seo-yoon at Lv5** | The only thing that matters as a joiner |
| Send max troops, marksman-heavy ratio | The bear doesn't fight back |
| Strong accounts lead, mid/lower accounts join | Leader contributes 9 skills; joiners contribute 1 |
| Cycle between rallies as troops return | Maximize 30-minute window |
| Activate Snow Leopard pet (+30% march speed) | Faster cycle = more rallies joined |
| Cyrille active for the event window | +30% damage to your output |
| **DO NOT solo attack the bear** | Damage will be 1-3% of rally damage |

## Troop Composition

Bear Trap uses generation-sensitive ratios. From the [Troop Ratios reference](/troops/ratios):

**Rally Leader (LEAD):** 0% Infantry / 10% Lancer / 90% Marksman

The bear has fixed HP and no counter triangle, so maximize lethality.

**Rally Joiner (JOIN):** 20% Infantry / 20% Lancer / 60% Marksman

Pre-Gen 8 balance point: some infantry to absorb hits and stabilize lancer/marksman skill triggers.

::: warning Generation-sensitive
**Pre-Gen 8 (covers most servers):** Keep at least 5,000 infantry in the lead march as a safety floor. The 0% lancer can stretch to 15–20% if your lancer lethality is close to marksman.

**Gen 8+:** Push to 0/15/85 or pure 0/10/90 for lead. Joiners shift to 10/15/75 as marksman lethality pulls ahead.
:::

## Best Heroes by Slot

### Rally Leader — Best Picks (2026)

The leader's full 9-skill kit applies, so all three heroes' Expedition skills matter. Pick heroes with damage-stacking skills.

**Infantry leads:** Jeronimo > Hector > Natalia > Flint > Magnus
**Lancer leads:** Dominic > Mia (with Exclusive Gear ≥6) > Karol > Sonya / Renee / Reina
**Marksman leads:** Newer-generation marksmen with strong base stats — most modern marksmen perform well here

For F2P-leaning rosters, **Hector has replaced Flint as the go-to F2P infantry rally lead** in 2026 — his second skill lets infantry heroes contribute damage to a marksman-heavy formation.

### Rally Joiner — Best Picks (Slot 1 only)

Only **+25% damage at Lv5** matters here. The S-tier joiners:

| Hero | Class | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| **Jessie** | Marksman (Epic) | The standard pick. Most accessible. |
| **Jeronimo** | Infantry (Mythic) | Same +25% damage skill |
| **Jasser** | Marksman (Mythic) | Same +25% damage skill |
| **Seo-yoon** | Marksman (Mythic) | Same +25% damage skill |

Tier-2 acceptable joiners (partial damage skills, not flat 25%): Philly, Reina, Gwen, Bradley.

**Avoid in slot 1:** Patrick, Sergey, Giny, Renee (lancer-only buffs), and any hero whose first skill buffs defense, HP, or only one troop type the bear doesn't engage with.
