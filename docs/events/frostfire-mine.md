# Frostfire Mine

::: tip
**Quick take:** Bi-weekly 30-minute solo PvP event. Teleport into a special map, occupy Orichalcum Veins, defeat Mine Patrols for XP, climb the Frostfire Skill Tree, and contest the Smelter at the end. Solo — even alliance members are enemies. Troops don't die. The recall-reoccupy trick lets F2P players score competitively without contesting the center. One of the best F2P-friendly competitive events in the game.
:::

## At a Glance

- **Frequency:** Bi-weekly (every 2 weeks)
- **Duration:** 30 minutes (3-minute prep + 27 minutes active competition)
- **Eligibility:** Furnace Lv. 16+
- **Time slots:** Choose one of seven daily windows: 02:00, 05:00, 11:00, 14:00, 16:00, 18:00, 21:00 UTC
- **Why it matters:** Charm Designs and Charm Guides for Chief Gear upgrades, Avatar Frames, gems, large resource bundles. Top-tier F2P players can hit Top 50 with the recall trick. Charm Designs are rare and primarily come from this event.

## How It Works

When the event opens, you select one of the daily time slots (every 4 hours) and register. At showtime, your city teleports to a special event map and you have 30 minutes to gather as much **Orichalcum** as possible.

**Key things to internalize before the event:**

- **It's solo PvP.** Even your own alliance members are enemies. There are no allies in Frostfire Mine.
- **Troops don't die.** You can't lose troops in this event. There's no risk to your army.
- **You have unlimited troops.** The game gives you infinite supply at your maximum trained tier.
- **You can't attack player cities.** All combat is over Veins and the Smelter.
- **Your Heroes carry their normal stats and gear** — that part isn't reset.

**The map has three zones:**

| Zone | Location | Vein levels | Risk |
|---|---|---|---|
| Outer ring | Edges of map | Lv 1 Veins (low yield, weak guards) | Low — fewer players |
| Middle ring | Between outer and center | Lv 2 Veins (medium yield) | Medium |
| Center | Map center | Lv 3 Veins (high yield, strong guards) + the Smelter | High — heavily contested |

### The Five Mining Targets

| Target | Capacity | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| **Lv 1 Veins** | 8,640 | Weak guards, low yield. Fine for low-power players. |
| **Lv 2 Veins** | 17,280 | Sweet spot for most F2P/mid-power players. |
| **Lv 3 Veins** | 34,560 | Highest regular yield. Heavily contested. |
| **Outburst Veins** | 4,000 (mined at +200/s = 20 sec) | **Spawn at minutes 8, 9, 10, 18, 19, 20.** No guards. **Always prioritize.** |
| **The Smelter** | Massive bonus pool | Opens at the 23-minute mark in Zone 3. PvP free-for-all. |

### Mine Patrols (Frostfire XP)

Mine Patrols are NPCs scattered across the map. Defeating them gives Frostfire XP, which unlocks the Skill Tree.

- **Lv 1 Patrols:** 75 XP each
- **Lv 2 Patrols:** 150 XP each
- **You need 5,400 total XP** to max the Skill Tree
- That's roughly 36 Lv 2 Patrols or 72 Lv 1 Patrols

**Patrol farming should be your first 7–10 minutes.** Don't start mining Veins until your Skill Tree is unlocked through Level 3 at minimum. The skill multipliers compound across the entire event — early skill investment pays back many times over.

### Vein Outbursts (the most important resource)

**Outbursts spawn at minutes 8, 9, 10, 18, 19, and 20.** A banner warns you 60 seconds and 10 seconds before each spawn.

Outburst Veins:
- Have **no guards** — easy to capture
- Mine at **+200 Orichalcum/second** (later boosted to ~230 with Lv 4 Left)
- Total capacity: 4,000 Orichalcum each
- Take 20 seconds to fully drain

These are the highest per-second yield in the event. **Always prioritize Outbursts over regular Vein gathering.** Recall your active marches when you see the 60-second warning banner.

### The Smelter

The Smelter unlocks at the **23-minute mark** in Zone 3 (center of the map). It's a PvP free-for-all — multiple players try to occupy it. Bonus Orichalcum is awarded based on total occupation time relative to other players.

**You don't need to hold the Smelter for long.** Even capturing it for 1–2 seconds puts you on the occupation ranking. For most F2P players, the recall trick (covered below) outscores Smelter contests in the final phase. For high-power spenders, contesting Smelter is the path to top-3 finishes.

## The Frostfire Skill Tree

The Skill Tree has 5 levels. At each level, you choose **Left or Right** — you cannot pick both. Choices are permanent for the event (resets at end). You can't switch paths mid-event.

### Skills by level

| Level | Left | Right |
|---|---|---|
| **Lv 1** | (Defensive — buff troops in your veins) | **+1,500 Orichalcum per Mine Patrol kill** |
| **Lv 2** | (Defensive option) | **+25% gathering speed (passive)** |
| **Lv 3** | **+5,000 Orichalcum on common Vein occupation (60s cooldown)** | (Alternative — defensive utility) |
| **Lv 4** | **+15% gathering efficiency (boosts Outburst yield to ~230/s)** | (Alternative) |
| **Lv 5** | **[Mine Armory] +66 Troop Damage for 60s, 5-min cooldown (PvP/Smelter)** | **+50 gathering speed for 60s, 5-min cooldown (Vein farming)** |

### Recommended Paths

**F2P / Hunt Path: Right → Right → Left → Left → Right**

Optimized for non-combat farming. Prioritizes patrol kills early, gathering speed mid-event, and the recall-reoccupy bonus throughout. The Lv 5 Right activates with vein outbursts.

**Spender / PvP Path: Left → Left → Left → Left → Left**

Optimized for combat — winning attacks against contested Veins, holding Lv 3 Veins, and contesting the Smelter at end. The Lv 5 Left damage boost is critical for Smelter PvP.

::: warning Skill choices are permanent for the event
You cannot reset the Skill Tree mid-event. Pick a path before you start. The "best F2P path" is consistent across community guides: **Right → Right → Left → Left → Right** for hunting and recall-trick farming.
:::

## The Recall-Reoccupy Trick (F2P signature technique)

The Lv 3 Left skill grants **+5,000 Orichalcum every time you successfully occupy a Vein**, with a 60-second internal cooldown.

**The exploit:**

1. Send 1 march to a nearby Lv 1 or Lv 2 Vein
2. Wait for occupation to complete and the +5,000 bonus to trigger
3. **Recall the march immediately** (before extracting much Orichalcum)
4. Wait until 60 seconds have passed since the last trigger
5. Send the same march back to the same Vein (or a different one) — triggers another +5,000

**Why it works:** The bonus is tied to the act of occupying, not to time spent gathering. Holding a Vein for 5 minutes gives you the +5,000 once. Recall-reoccupying every 60 seconds gives you the +5,000 five times in the same 5 minutes.

**Yield over a full 25 minutes of active play: ~100,000–125,000 Orichalcum from this trick alone.**

::: tip Practical execution notes
- Your other marches can simultaneously gather, hunt patrols, or grab Outbursts — the recall trick uses only 1 march
- During Outburst windows (minutes 8, 9, 10, 18, 19, 20), suspend the recall trick and grab Outbursts instead
- The skill has no visible cooldown indicator. Use the event timer at the top of the screen to track 60-second intervals
- The Lv3 Right alternative also rewards 5,000 on **failed** occupations — same value, but Left is more reliable
:::

## Strategy by Player Type

### F2P / Low Power (20M–40M)

- **Stick to Lv 1 and Lv 2 Veins.** Don't try to take Lv 3 Veins from whales.
- **First 7–10 minutes:** patrol farming for XP. Aim to unlock Lv 3 Skill Tree.
- **Once Lv 3 Left unlocked:** start the recall-reoccupy trick. Use 1 march for the trick, others for patrol farming and Outbursts.
- **Avoid the Smelter.** It's whale territory at 23+ minute mark. Stay in your zone and farm.
- **Realistic target: 80,000–150,000 Orichalcum.**

### Mid Power (50M–80M)

- **Same patrol-first opening.** Lv 3 Skill Tree unlock is mandatory.
- **Prioritize Lv 2 Veins and Outbursts.** Lv 3 Veins are contested but reachable.
- **Recall-reoccupy trick still applies.** It's the highest-leverage F2P-friendly technique at any power level.
- **Optional:** late-event Smelter raid for the bonus ranking — even 1–2 seconds of Smelter occupation adds to your final reward.
- **Realistic target: 150,000–250,000 Orichalcum.**

### High Power (100M+)

- **Contest Lv 3 Veins early.** Take and hold the highest-yield regular Veins from competitors.
- **Smelter contention at 23 minutes.** Stay in PvP fights for occupation time.
- **Outbursts are the same 4,000-each cap regardless of power** — even whales must rotate through them.
- **Aggressive skill path:** Left → Left → Left → Left → Left for combat dominance.
- **Realistic target: 350,000+ Orichalcum, top 10 finish achievable.**

## Phase by Phase

### Pre-Event (3-Minute Prep Phase)

- **Activate gem-purchased buffs.** 10% attack/defense/march speed buffs are active in Frostfire Mine. (Note: Presidential appointments do NOT apply.)
- **Activate gathering bonuses from items, pets, and skins.** These DO apply.
- **Scout the map.** Find a spot in Zone 1 or 2 with patrol spawn points and 2+ Lv 2 Veins nearby. Teleport there before the battle starts.
- **Verify all your troops are inside your settlement** before the event. Troops out gathering or marching when the event starts won't be available.

::: warning March speed bonuses don't carry in
Your existing March Speed and Wilderness March Speed bonuses are **disabled** inside Frostfire Mine. The exception is March Speed bonuses granted by Frostfire Skills (which work normally).
:::

### Minutes 0–3 (Setup)

- Send all marches to attack the nearest Mine Patrols
- Unlock Lv 1 skill — pick **Right (+1,500 Orichalcum per Patrol)** for the F2P/Hunt path
- Continue hunting patrols, looting any dropped Orichalcum Piles

### Minutes 3–7 (XP Farming)

- Pure Mine Patrol focus
- Unlock Lv 2 skill — Right (+25% gathering speed) for F2P, Left for PvP
- Send 1–2 marches to start occupying Lv 1 or Lv 2 Veins for passive Orichalcum generation
- **Use Advance Teleport (free with 6-min cooldown)** to reposition if your area is depleted

### Minutes 7–10 (Outburst Window 1)

- At 7:00, recall all gathering marches in preparation
- **At 8:00, 9:00, and 10:00 — Outbursts spawn.** Send marches to grab them (4,000 Orichalcum at +200/s = 20-second mining)
- After 10:00, return to patrol farming and Vein occupation

### Minutes 10–17 (Mid-Event)

- Unlock Lv 3 Skill Tree — **Left (+5,000 on occupation, 60s cooldown)** is the critical F2P pick
- **Start the recall-reoccupy trick.** Every 60 seconds, recall a Vein-occupying march, then send it back (or to another Vein).
- Continue patrol farming with other marches

### Minutes 17–20 (Outburst Window 2)

- At 17:00, recall marches in preparation
- **At 18:00, 19:00, and 20:00 — Outbursts spawn again.** Same as Window 1.
- Suspend the recall trick during Outburst windows.

### Minutes 20–23 (Pre-Smelter)

- Resume recall-reoccupy trick
- Unlock Lv 4 Left (+15% gathering efficiency, boosts Outbursts to ~230/s)
- Position 1–2 marches near Zone 3 if you plan to contest the Smelter

### Minutes 23–30 (Smelter Phase)

**For F2P:** Continue recall-reoccupy. The trick outscores Smelter contention at lower power levels.

**For mid-power:** Send 1 march to brush the Smelter for occupation time (even 1–2 seconds counts). Other marches keep recall-trick farming.

**For high-power:** Full Smelter assault. Use Lv 5 Left damage skill to hold position.

**Final 2 minutes:** Rush to occupy unguarded Veins as competitors abandon positions for the Smelter. Free Orichalcum.

### Event End

- Rewards mailed within 24 hours based on final ranking
- **Frostfire XP and Skill Tree reset for the next event** — every event starts fresh

## Common Mistakes

1. **Mining before unlocking skills.** Spending the first 5 minutes occupying Veins instead of farming patrols means you reach the recall-reoccupy phase too late. The 36-patrol XP grind is the foundation of every other strategy.

2. **Picking the wrong skill path.** The Skill Tree cannot be reset for the event. F2P players who pick all-Left (combat path) and then can't compete for Lv 3 Veins or the Smelter are stuck with worse rewards.

3. **Ignoring Outbursts.** The 6 Outburst spawns (minutes 8/9/10/18/19/20) are the highest per-second yield in the entire event. Missing them costs ~24,000 Orichalcum potential.

4. **Going to the center of the map as a low-power player.** Lv 3 Veins look attractive (34,560 capacity vs 8,640) but they're whale territory. You'll lose your occupation in seconds. Stay in your zone.

5. **Not using the recall-reoccupy trick.** Even casual F2P players benefit massively from this. The 60-second cycle is mechanical — set a mental timer.

6. **Forgetting that troops respawn instantly.** "I don't want to lose troops" instinct is wrong here. Always send full marches. You have unlimited troops at your max-trained tier.

7. **Trying to use Presidential buffs.** They don't work inside Frostfire Mine. Don't burn them in the prep phase.

8. **Letting your settlement march speed buffs influence strategy.** They're disabled inside the mine. The only march speed boosts that work come from the Skill Tree itself.

9. **Forgetting to register for the time slot.** Multiple time slots per day, but you must register. Forgetting means missing the event entirely.

10. **Recalling Outburst marches too early.** Outburst Veins drain in 20 seconds. Stay until empty. The few seconds of "nothing" between recalls is a minor inefficiency vs. the massive yield.
