# Events Hub

Every timed event in Whiteout Survival, organized by frequency. Each guide follows the same structure: at-a-glance summary, mechanics, scoring, phase-by-phase actions, common mistakes, and a quick reference card.

## Currently Available

### State of Power (SvS)
**Monthly · Cross-state war**

The most rewarding event in the game. 5.5 days of preparation scoring against a matched enemy state, followed by a 12-hour battle phase, then 48 hours of troop revival.

- [SvS Overview & Strategy](/events/svs/overview)
- [SvS Prep Checklist](/events/svs/prep-checklist)

### Bear Trap
**Twice-weekly · Alliance PvE event**

Rally your alliance against a Raging Bear that doesn't fight back — zero troop losses, pure reward event. Essence Stones, XP Components, and hero-gear materials drop based on damage dealt. The single most important mechanic: as a joiner, only your slot 1 hero's first Expedition skill matters.

- [Bear Trap Guide](/events/bear-trap)

### Fishing Tournament
**Monthly · Solo mini-game**

A casual 3-day fishing mini-game with no combat or troop risk. Permanent stat-boost skins from collection completion (+2% Troop Attack, +2% Troop Defense) make it one of the best free buff sources in the game — accessible to F2P players at any progression level.

- [Fishing Tournament Guide](/events/fishing-tournament)

### Snowbusters
**Monthly · Solo mini-game**

Control Buster the snowplow through 25 progressively harder stages. Manage Fuel, rescue Survivors, and defeat Beasts across 3 days. Permanent rewards include themed cosmetic skins and a unique island building unlocked at the 20-star Map Fragment collection milestone.

- [Snowbusters Guide](/events/snowbusters)

### Canyon Clash
**Monthly · Alliance vs. alliance vs. alliance battle**

The most coordination-intensive event in the game. Top-20 alliances only. Three alliances fight simultaneously in the Lost Canyon map over six days (vote → register → matchmake → Saturday battle). One hour of live combat across three stages — Seize & Conquer, Fortress Occupation, and Citadel Onslaught — with Fuel management and voice chat coordination determining winners.

- [Canyon Clash Guide](/events/canyon-clash)

### Alliance Championship
**Weekly · Cross-server alliance tournament**

Weekly cross-server tournament where six alliances of similar tier compete in automated three-lane battles — no active play required during combat. Zero troop losses. All the value comes from registration optimization (50/20/30 troop ratio, buff capture before clicking Register) and R4/R5 lane assignment after matchmaking.

- [Alliance Championship Guide](/events/alliance-championship)

### Crazy Joe
**Bi-weekly · Alliance defense event**

Bi-weekly 40-minute alliance defense where 20 waves of AI bandits hit your cities, with three Elite waves (7, 14, 17) that double points and only target online players. One of the best F2P sources of Chief Gear materials — the trade-troops trick (swap Infantry/Lancers with a partner before Wave 1) roughly doubles both players' scores.

- [Crazy Joe Guide](/events/crazy-joe)

### Foundry Battle
**Bi-weekly · Alliance vs. alliance facility war**

Bi-weekly one-hour two-alliance battle on a contained map for control of 11 buildings across three phases. The Imperial Foundry is the centerpiece (9,000 capture + 1,800/minute hold); rallies are allowed, unlike Canyon Clash. Arsenal Tokens fund the Foundry Shop for Charm Designs, Hero Shards, and gear materials.

- [Foundry Battle Guide](/events/foundry-battle)

### Frostfire Mine
**Bi-weekly · Solo PvP gathering event**

Bi-weekly 30-minute solo event where even alliance mates are enemies — occupy Orichalcum Veins, farm Mine Patrols for Skill Tree XP, and master the recall-reoccupy trick (Lv 3 Left skill, +5,000 per occupation) for 100,000+ bonus Orichalcum. One of the best F2P sources of Charm Designs for Chief Gear upgrades.

- [Frostfire Mine Guide](/events/frostfire-mine)

### Hall of Chiefs
**Bi-weekly · Pre-SvS resource-spending competition**

Bi-weekly scoring competition for Generation 1 and 2 states that haven't yet completed SvS. Seven daily themes — using items on the wrong day scores zero — building the stockpile discipline and daily-theme awareness that transfer directly to King of Icefield and SvS Prep. Permanently replaced by KoI after your state's first SvS.

- [Hall of Chiefs Guide](/events/hall-of-chiefs)

### King of Icefield
**Monthly · Cross-state scoring event**

Seven-day cross-state scoring event that replaces Hall of Chiefs after your state's first SvS. Six states matched, five reward streams, 14 Medals of Honor available across the week. Match resources to their scoring day, secure Tier 4 milestones before chasing rankings, and save Mithril for Day 5 — the consistently highest-point day.

- [King of Icefield Guide](/events/king-of-icefield)

### Mercenary Prestige
**Monthly · Solo and alliance stamina event**

Monthly 50-wave gauntlet against escalating Phaethon Mercenaries, plus alliance Captain bosses for shared rewards. Failed solo attacks refund their stamina, making experimentation nearly free — only the 15 scout stamina is at risk. Gina's Endurance Training (up to 20% stamina reduction) is the key F2P-friendly mechanic, saving ~200 stamina per event.

- [Mercenary Prestige Guide](/events/mercenary-prestige)

### Sunfire Castle Battle
**Biweekly · Intra-state castle battle**

Biweekly 5-hour state-internal battle where all alliances are enemies — whoever holds the Sunfire Castle for 2.5 consecutive hours (or the longest cumulative hold) wins, and the alliance R5 becomes State President. Troops are wounded not killed, keeping participation costs low. The mechanics transfer directly to SvS Castle Battle.

- [Sunfire Castle Battle Guide](/events/sunfire-castle)

## Where to Start

**New to SvS?** Read the [SvS Overview](/events/svs/overview) front to back. It's the longest guide on the site, but SvS is the most rewarding event and the most punishing if you don't know the mechanics.

**Mid-event reference?** Use the [Prep Checklist](/events/svs/prep-checklist) — designed to be tapped through on mobile while you're actively playing.

**Looking for a specific mechanic?** Use the search box at the top — "polar terror," "field triage," "rebirth tomes" all jump to the right page.
